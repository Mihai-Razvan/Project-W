ActionLock: 
-"UNLOCKED": can do anything
-"ACTION_LOCKED": you can't do interactions (can't open invenotry, chests, open inventory, settings, but you can move), used while using an item (ex grappler)
-"UI_OPENED": player inventory, chest inventory, menu, other building ui (can't move, rotate camera, interact with buildings, use items, open other ui, just close
the opened one)
